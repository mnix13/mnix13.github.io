Mirepoix

Appearance
Description Mirepoix is a classic base for French cuisine . It is composed of a blend of celery, carrot, and onion with a 1:1:2 ratio. It is a sweet base, usually addded to stocks/soups/sauces to enhance the flavor (in comparison to plain water). Mirepoix is a great way to add a flare to your favorite dish.
Ingredients
  • Butter or oil
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
Recipe
  1. Chop up your ingredients based into chunks, based on your cook time. For shorter cooking times, use smaller pieces.
  2. Heat your pan to a medium heat
  3. Add your butter or oil and give it time to heat up
  4. Add your ingredients and let them saute.
  5. Transfer them out the pan, and add them to whatever you'd like (a pot to make a stock, or etc.).
