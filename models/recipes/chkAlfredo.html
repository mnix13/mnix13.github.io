Chicken Alfredo

Appearance
Course Main Entr√©e
Description Chicken Alfredo is a popular pasta dish within the Italian Cuisines. It can be served with or without chicken, however including a meat portion is often encouraged.
Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Parsley
  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • Alfredo Sauce
Recipe
  1. Grill Chicken
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil, then add fettuccine noodles and cook until softened
  3. Drain water from noodles.
  4. Combine cheese, Alfredo sauce, and noodles together
  5. Serve with chicken on top of noodles or mixed throughout
  6. Sprinkle parsley over dish before serving
