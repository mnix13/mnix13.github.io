Pico de Gallo

Appearance
Course Appetizer
Description Pico de gallo is used in much the same way as other Mexican cuisine liquid salsas, but since it contains less liquid, it can also be used as a main ingredient in dishes such as tacos and fajitas.
Ingredients
  • Cilantro
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Lime juice
Recipe
  1. Remove cilantro from stem and add to large bowl
  2. Dice your tomato, onion, and jalapeno into approximately 1cm x 1cm chunks
  3. Add diced tomato, onion, and jalapeno to bowl and stir thoroughly
  4. Squeeze lime juice over mixture to taste
  5. Serve with chips!
